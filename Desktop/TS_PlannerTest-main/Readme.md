# Domain: Software Development / Product Engineering

---

## Business Process: Weekly Work Planning and Tracking for Asynchronous Business Systems Building Agent

---

### Phase 1: Backlog Management

1. Team members add activities to the backlog at any time.
2. System classifies each activity as "client-focused," "tech-debt," or "R&D."

---

### Phase 2: Weekly Planning Preparation

3. On Tuesday, the team convenes for planning session.
4. A rotating team member assumes the role of planning lead for the session.
5. Planning lead facilitates the prioritization of backlog activities within each classification category.

---

### Phase 3: Capacity Calculation

6. Team members mark their unavailability for the upcoming work period (Wednesday through Monday).
7. System calculates total available hours per team member based on 8-hour workdays minus marked unavailability.
8. System allocates minimum capacity targets: 50% to "client-focused," 25% to "tech-debt," and 25% to "R&D."

---

### Phase 4: Work Assignment

9. Team members select activities from the prioritized backlog.
10. Team members assign selected activities to themselves in 1-hour segments.
11. Team members allocate hours to meet or exceed the minimum percentage targets for each classification category.
12. Team members may allocate total hours exceeding 100% of their calculated available capacity.

---

### Phase 5: Plan Finalization

13. System monitors completion status as team members fill their weekly allocation.
14. When all team members reach 100% allocation, the system publishes the weekly plan.

---

### Phase 6: Execution and Tracking

15. Team members execute assigned activities during the work period (Wednesday through Monday).
16. Team members track and record progress against the published plan.
